What are temples?

The temple is the house of the Lord. In temples, God's children draw nearer to Him and His Son Jesus Christ by making sacred promises with Him. Temples are the only places on earth where families can be united eternally. Those who worship in the house of the Lord receive peace, inspiration, joy, and spiritual power.

Where are temples located?

Temples are located all around the world. Since the construction of the first temple in Kirtland, Ohio in 1836, The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints has built temples to serve their global membership. The Church current has 350 temples operating, under construction, or announced and planned to be built.
